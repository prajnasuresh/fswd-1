A tribute to frontline corona warriors: Doctors and Healthcare workers who sacrificed their lives
while saving patients during the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic.

A tribute to the real unsung heroes of the pandemic


It’s been around one-and-a-half years since the Covid pandemic engulfed the whole country and no one knows when the virus will fade away. In these torrid times, our doctors and nurses have been serving day and night, like the soldiers who guard our borders, to protect millions of lives.

From being witnesses to so many deaths around them to working tirelessly without a break to getting themselves self-quarantined due to the fear of taking the virus home, the doctors and nursing staff were driven to the breaking point many a times during the pandemic.

It has been an emotionally and physically exhausting journey for doctors and healthcare-workers – both at the personal and professional level.

In order to show our respect and express gratitude towards them we continue to celebrate them for their unconditional service.
